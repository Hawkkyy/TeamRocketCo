About Team Rocket Co.


We are a Card Trader Shop aimed at delivering our customers the highest quality Base Set Pokémon TCG Cards. First started our Business in 1996, we have been steadily but surely expanding our reach to all corners of the globe.


To keep up with the new innovations and trends of today, we upgraded our system to include a database that:

  • Keeps Track of our Cards
  • Supports Online Ordering
  • Defines User Privileges
  • Generates Reports for Cards and Orders as PDF

Our newly launched website offers many features, including viewing our card stock and ordering directly from the site!
